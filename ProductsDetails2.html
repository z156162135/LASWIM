BDP Series High Performance Pump

BDP series high performance plastic centrifugal pump delivers high flow rates. It's designed

for the commercial swimming pool, spa, waterfall and water features to achieve the maxi

mum working efficiency with the lowest noise. Suitable for operation with salt chlorinators and sea water.

Features

- High efficiency motors IE3

- IPX5 waterproof standard

- Asynchronous , two poles

- Insulation class: F

- The advanced diffuser and high efficiency turbine maximize water flow and energy efficiency while minimizing turbulence and noise

- Self-priming up to 3M above water level

- Built-in thermal protection and overload protection

- The oversized pre-filter basket (5L) extends the interval between cleanings
